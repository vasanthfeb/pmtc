About the College

Pasumpon Muthuramalinga Thevar College, Affiliated to Madurai Kamaraj University, was started with fund of the kallar community and is administered by the KALLAR KALVI KAZHAGAM. The Long –time dream of people of the area, to have a college, came true by the priceless effort of the P.K.MOOKIAH THEVAR B.A.M.P & M.L.A ,V.K.C. NATARAJAN, IAS and other well-wishers of the community.


The contribution made by farmers, the teachers of kallar and panchayat union schools and the common funds of the PIRAMALAI KALLAR community provided assistance to start this institution. The college is situated on the Madurai – Theni National highway at a distance of 3 Kms from Usilampatti Town. It covers an area of 54.65acres. The college was inaugurated on 05.07.1968 By Dr.T.P .Meenakshi Sundaram , The then Vice Chancellor of Madurai Kamaraj University,Madurai.

Vision & Mission

Empowering students with knowledge and skills for sports, fitness, and well-being in a supportive environment.

We believe in the power of youth to teach life lessons that build Stronger Individuals and Society.Through Service and Leadership Opportunities, we will equip the students to make our society a better place for upcoming generation.
